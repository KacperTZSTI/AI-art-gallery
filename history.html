Proffesional AI reaserch began in the 1950's, when in 1956 it was founded as an academic discipline. The first significant AI art system was AARON, created by Harold Cohenin the late 1960's at the University of California at San Diego. It used a basic, rule-based approach to generate images. It created simple, black and white drawings for Cohen to finish.
